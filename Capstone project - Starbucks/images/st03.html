Starbucks

In 1971 the first Starbucks store was opened. Since then, they have not left aside their purpose: “share the best coffee with our friends and contribute to making the world a little better”.

Starbucks: a café with a lot of history and tradition

At that time, the company was made up of a single store in Seattle, in the historic market of Pike Place Market. Starbucks has been offering since 1971 the best freshly roasted coffee beans in the world.

The name with which it was decided to mint the brand is due to the novel Moby Dick, which evokes the romanticism of the high seas and the maritime tradition of the first coffee merchants. The Starbucks logo is a mermaid with two tails inspired by an illustration of a Norwegian book of the fifteenth century. If you want to know more about the history of Starbucks, you can check its website in which they explain each of the details of how they started making and selling their coffees, their history and how they have evolved since then.

Starbucks not only sells coffees, in its stores you can also find hot drinks, snacks, the raw material with which they create their products and merchandising as glasses or cups.

A coffee that is present around the world

The Starbucks franchise is known around the world for the quality of its coffee. It currently has more than 15,000 stores in 50 countries. Its mission is “to inspire and nourish the human spirit, only one person, one glass and one neighborhood at the same time”. You have to take into account what the American company has achieved. It has turned the fact of drinking coffee into a lifestyle.

At TS-Spain we have several of their tastiest drinks with sugar and without sugar. You can buy the Doubleshot espresso version with sugar, Doubleshot espresso without sugar, Discoveries Skinny Latte without lactose, Chocolate Signature, Cappucchino, Frapuccino Coffee, Frappuccino Vanilla, Discoveries Seattle Latte and Caramel Macchiato. Any of the options of its coffees is perfect to drink it cold. These products are ideal to drink in summer because they combine the refreshing touch of a cold drink and the stimulant of a good coffee. These coffees are inspired by classic Starbucks drinks.

In these ready-to-drink beverages, you will find all the flavor and essence of Starbucks Original. The taste of freshly roasted coffee without having to move from home. Starbucks coffee stands out for its particular flavor and its unmistakable aroma. It is an excellent option for any time of the day. It has a special toasting that makes it considered a high quality coffee.
